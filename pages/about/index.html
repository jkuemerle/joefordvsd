About Me

My name is Joe Kuemerle and I am running for a spot as a Director for the Delaware Valley School Board. I have been a resident of Pike County since 2014 and two of my children have graduated from DVSD.

With my skills as a cybersecurity engineer I bring a deep knowledge of technolgy, how to best use it for education and how to provide a secure digital environment for learning.

As a community member and active volunteer I will work to include the Delaware Valley School District into the community.

I have served as the Milford Township Judge of Elections since 2023, in which I have helped to ensure the secuirty and integrity of all votes cast in Milford Township.

As the Vice President and instructor of the non-profit Milford Tae Kwon Do I have worked to bring the training and discipline of martial arts to children and adults of our community.

As volunteer organizer of the Techbash Conference in Mt. Pocono, PA I have built trainings and cirriculum to expand children and families userstanding of technology and programming.

Please reach out if you have any questions.
